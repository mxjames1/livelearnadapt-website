Where to Find These Products

Most of these items can be found at Whole Foods. Increasingly, places like Target and your local grocery stores carry them as well.

If you can't find what you're looking for locally, ask your store to stock them! The only way to increase supply is to create demand.

For hard-to-find items or to discover new products, check out Vegan Essentials. It's a bit expensive but worth every penny for the selection.
