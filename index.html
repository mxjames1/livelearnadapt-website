Start Your Journey

The best thing you can do to make your transition into a vegan lifestyle a success is to watch the documentaries Earthlings and Dominion. Both films will remove any doubt in your mind that you've made the right choice. The truth in each of those documentaries cannot be unseen or forgotten.

Watch Earthlings Watch Dominion

The second best thing you can do is understand that you simply need to make a few minor changes to have a major impact. Being vegan isn't about being perfect. It's about doing your best with the information available and trying to reduce the amount of suffering, pain, and destruction in the world.

Take It One Step at a Time

For people who are new to a vegan lifestyle, I always recommend trying to limit the amount of change you take on at once. This is especially true when starting to change your diet.

A Promise From Experience

As a lifelong meat and potatoes kinda guy, I can assure you that you will not miss ANY food while adopting a vegan lifestyle. If you must have a specific food, then by all means eat that food. Just don't allow this one thing to prevent you from being vegan all the other times.

After a while of being vegan, I guarantee that you will no longer want to eat that non-vegan dish ever again.
